The App

TapShorts connects Flic 2 Bluetooth buttons to iOS Shortcuts. The app allows you to assign shortcuts to button press actions (single press, double press, and long press) so that pressing a physical button triggers an iOS Shortcut on your device.

Requirements

  • An iPhone running iOS 16.0 or later
  • A Flic 2 Bluetooth button (sold separately at flic.io)
  • The Apple Shortcuts app (built into iOS)

Free and Pro Tiers

TapShorts is free to download and use with the following limitations:
